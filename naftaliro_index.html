about this place
who is naftali?▾
Naftali is my Hebrew name — the one people who actually know me use. Online I go by kestrel or 3hz. This site exists in between: not quite public-facing, not quite private. Just honest. The professional version of me is at mitchellberg.org.
why a separate site from 3hz.dev?▾
3hz.dev is outward-facing — the alias, the security work, the technical identity. This site is for the things that don't fit there: writing, what I'm reading, what I'm thinking about. Softer. Less structured. More me.
what does "naftaliro" mean?▾
Naftali is a Hebrew name meaning "my struggle" or "one who wrestles." The "ro" is just what made the handle unique enough to register. I like that the root word implies effort — like the wrestling isn't failure, it's the point.
